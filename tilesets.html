Intro

This algorithm is designed to arrange different tiles together with the goal of creating an image that is aesthetically pleasing.
The input images are a total of 5 square tiles in a total of 12 orientations. The output image is an arangement of these tiles such that they logically fit together like pieces of a puzzle.

The Tiles

To distinguish one tileset from another, the naming convention "TileName[TileSetName].png" is used. All lines on the tiles need to be centered, or the final result
will appear choppy. If you make me a tileset and send it to me at @ethantomb25 on twitter, it will be live on the website within 24 hours.
